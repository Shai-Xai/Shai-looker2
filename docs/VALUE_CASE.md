## 1. Executive Summary

Today, almost every recurring account-management task is performed across
**three or more disconnected systems** (Looker, Howler, plus manual exports into
Meta / TikTok). Each handoff costs an Account Manager (AM) time, introduces
error, and — in several cases — is simply **not possible at all** without a
manual workaround.

**Pulse** collapses these workflows into a **single governed platform** where the
audiences and campaigns run on the **same data that drives the dashboards**.
Tasks that take **hours** today are completed in **minutes**, and several tasks
that are **impossible today** become **live and automatic**.

> **Bottom line:** Onboarding and servicing a client drops from a **multi-hour,
> multi-tool ordeal** to a **few minutes inside one platform**. The capacity
> freed up lets each AM service **dramatically more clients** without adding
> headcount.

## 4. Why This Matters Beyond the Clock

The time savings are the headline, but three structural shifts matter just as
much:

1. **From impossible to standard.** Per-client branding, custom-list email,
   paid-social audience sync, and multi-campaign reporting weren't slow before —
   they **didn't exist**. Pulse turns them into one-click, repeatable features.

2. **From reactive to proactive.** The daily email no longer just reports — it
   **recommends actions**. AMs move from data janitors to strategic advisors.

3. **From fragmented to unified.** Eliminating the Looker → Howler → export →
   Meta/TikTok handoffs removes the **error surface** entirely. Live segments and
   live reporting mean no stale exports, no re-import mistakes, no version drift.

---

## 5. The Capacity Argument

If a full servicing cycle drops from ~11 hours to ~30 minutes, a single AM's
effective capacity increases by roughly **20×** on these tasks. In practice this
means:

- **Onboard more clients** without hiring.
- **Reallocate AM time** from manual assembly to client strategy and upsell.
- **Offer new paid services** (branded dashboards, retargeting audiences,
  proactive insights) that were previously not deliverable.

Pulse doesn't just make the current job faster — it **changes what the job is**.
