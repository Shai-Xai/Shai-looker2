Where this lives

Three realistic placements for the same animation system - one build, tuned per surface.

Placement 01Landing-page hero

Runs full-bleed behind the headline on the public Pulse page, pointer-events off, at ~60% of this intensity. The scenario auto-rotates every 20s so the page never looks the same twice.

Placement 02In-app loading & empty states

A single-stream slice of the same system (one source → Owl → one surface) replaces spinners while dashboards hydrate - the wait itself tells the product story.

Placement 03Sales deck & wizard finale

Full interactive version with the scenario tabs, used live in pitches and as the closing screen of the client setup wizard - "this is what we just switched on for you."

Implementation notes: one <canvas> for the flowing streams + real Pulse screenshots with DOM alert cards floating on top - no libraries, self-contained. Insight particles route through the action they trigger (alert / digest / campaign / segment / report) before landing on a surface; cyan particles are questions flowing INTO the Owl. Respects prefers-reduced-motion, throttles when the tab is hidden, collapses to a vertical flow on phones. Screenshots are the real Pulse UI; figures are illustrative.
