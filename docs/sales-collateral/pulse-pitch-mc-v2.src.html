On the ground

Event Ops · your operations command centre

A live overview of how the whole event is flowing: the gates, the bars, the devices, the data itself. Replaces the clipboard, the spreadsheet and the frantic WhatsApp group with one phone-first console.

🗺 The live overview: one board with the whole operation: device counts by state, per-station deployment, open issues and a live activity feed. The "where is everything right now" view.
📟 Every device tracked: scanners, payment devices, radios, printers: bulk-register a batch, then scan to move between the store and stations. Every move lands in a tamper-proof audit trail, nothing is lost to memory, and every device is accounted for at event close.
🔥 Hotspots & the heat map: every station's activity in time blocks: watch the crowd move from gates to bars, spot the overloaded station and the dead one, staff accordingly.
🍹 Bar & operator performance: every bar and every bartender, live: sales and rands-per-hour per operator, who's flying and which till has gone quiet. Praise the stars at the debrief; fix the slow lane before the queue grows.
📶 Connectivity & data health: is data from every station actually arriving? Minutes-since-last-record per station with alerting when a stream goes quiet; a device roster showing exactly which units are offline and for how long. You'll know a scanner died before the queue does.
🩺 AI Diagnose + event report: one-tap AI verdicts per station, and a post-event diagnostics report clean enough to forward to the network provider.
⚡ Live updates & alerts: compact multi-metric updates while doors are open (gates pace, bar revenue, top vendors, device health) straight to your phone, plus threshold alerts on any number that matters.
