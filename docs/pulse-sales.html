Know

No more digging through dashboards hoping to spot the story. Pulse opens on a personalised briefing of what moved and why it matters. And when a number crosses a line, it finds you.

Dashboards that explain themselves

Live KPIs, tables and charts on your real data. Tap any tile and the Owl tells you what the numbers mean, in plain English.

The important stuff comes to you

A tap on the shoulder, not a vigil

On your phone, properly

The Owl

One brain. Every channel.

The Owl is Pulse's AI data analyst, one that has already read your live event data before you ask. Ask it anything in plain language ("how are VIP sales tracking vs last year?") and it answers with your real numbers, charts included. It never guesses, and it can only ever see your data.

It knows your numbers

Grounded in the same governed data as your dashboards: every answer comes from a live query of your ticketing, cashless and web data, never from thin air.

It does, not just tells

Ask it to watch a number, build an audience or draft a campaign, and it sets the alert, saves the segment, writes and designs the email. Drafts always wait for your approval; the Owl never sends.

It remembers

Your business, each event's context, and how you personally like your answers, so every conversation starts smarter than the last.

Act

From “interesting” to “sent” in one sitting.

Most tools stop at the chart. Pulse's audiences and campaigns run on the same live data as the dashboards, so the moment you spot a cohort worth talking to, you can reach them. No exports, no CSV gymnastics, no third tool.

Audiences that stay live

Build a segment from a dashboard tile, a spreadsheet or a Google Sheet (combine, intersect or exclude lists) and it re-resolves itself at send time.

Campaigns that look like you

Email and SMS in your brand, with AI-drafted copy and design, personalised to any column, promo codes on the buy link.

Automations that catch people in the act

Abandoned-cart journeys that enrol people in real time, stop when they buy, and show you exactly where each step converts.

Safe by design

Consent-aware (POPIA), one-click unsubscribe, approval gates before anything sends, and per-client send caps: speed without the sweat.

Prove

Results, not vibes.

Tell Pulse what success looks like and every screen shows how you're tracking: against your target, against last time, and against where you'll actually land.

Goals with a pace read

Ahead, on track or behind, read off your last event's real sell-curve, so a late-loading show isn't cried "behind" too early.

A forecast you can follow

Every goal projects its final landing from real curve shapes, so you know you'll fall short while there's still time to act.

Every send accounted for

Opens, clicks and conversions tracked per recipient and per journey step; campaign results flow straight back into your dashboards.

The loop closes itself

What worked shapes the next briefing, the next digest, the next suggested action. Pulse gets sharper the longer you use it.
