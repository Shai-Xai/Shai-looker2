02 · What it costs

By the time the campaign ships,
the moment is gone.

Missed opportunities

The signal goes cold

A surge, a stall, an abandoned cart — spotted Monday, briefed Wednesday, approved Friday. The customer moved on days ago.

Rigid calendars

Locked a quarter out

Campaigns planned months ahead can't react to what's actually happening. The plan gets executed whether the market agrees or not.

Vanilla messaging

One message, everyone

The superfan on their fifth year, the first-timer, the person who abandoned checkout last night — all got the same email. Personalisation was a first name, at best.

03 · The shift

When live data, AI analysis and campaign execution live in one system, the distance from customer signal → response collapses from weeks to minutes. Marketing stops being a broadcast schedule and becomes a living system that responds.

  • Act — the campaign is drafted, targeted and ready in the same place.
  • Learn — results flow straight back in and sharpen the next move.

05 · Reliable, instant insight

Answers in seconds,
not reports in weeks.

  • Live dashboards on your real ticketing, cashless and web data — drill from headline to detail, on your phone.
  • Ask the Owl anything in plain language — "what's selling right now?", "how does this compare to last year?" — answered from your own data, in-app or on WhatsApp.

06 · Segmentation

Audiences that build themselves —
and never go stale.

  • Built from your live data — turn any dashboard cohort into a named audience; filter on any field: ticket type, city, age, purchase history.
  • Combine like sets — union, intersect, exclude. "Abandoned cart, minus everyone we already called."
  • Always-live — segments re-resolve at the moment of send. New matches flow in, buyers drop out. No exported snapshot ever goes stale.
  • Scoped and governed — locked to your events, on the same server-enforced data boundary as everything else in Pulse.

11 · Journeys, not blasts

Every customer's path is different.
Now every journey is too.

12 · Speed, safely

Signal to send in minutes —
with a human hand on the gate.

  • One-tap drafts — the Owl turns an insight into a targeted, on-brand, ready-to-review campaign straight from the briefing.
  • Nothing sends itself — every campaign passes an explicit approval gate, routed to the named people you choose.
  • Consent, enforced — per-channel marketing consent (GDPR-ready) checked at send time, one-click unsubscribe, always.
  • Caps and audit — send caps put a hard ceiling on reach and cost, and every send is tracked per recipient.
