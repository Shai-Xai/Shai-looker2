On the ground

Event Ops — your operations command centre

A live overview of how the whole event is flowing — the gates, the bars, the devices, the data itself. Replaces the clipboard, the spreadsheet and the frantic WhatsApp group with one phone-first console.

🗺 The live overview — one board with the whole operation: device counts by state, per-station deployment, open issues and a live activity feed. The "where is everything right now" view.
📟 Every device tracked — scanners, payment devices, radios, printers: bulk-register a batch, then scan to move between the store and stations. Every move lands in an append-only audit trail — nothing is lost to memory, and every device is accounted for at event close.
🔥 Hotspots & the heat map — every station's activity through the day in time blocks: watch the crowd move from gates to bars, spot the station that's overloaded and the one that's dead — and staff accordingly, tonight and next year.
📶 Connectivity & data health — is data from every station actually arriving? Minutes-since-last-record per station with alerting when a stream goes quiet; a device roster showing exactly which units are offline and for how long. You'll know a scanner died before the queue does.
🩺 AI Diagnose + event report — one tap per station gets an AI verdict with ranked concerns and a suggested fix; after the event, an AI-drafted diagnostics report for the debrief — with a connectivity section clean enough to forward to the network provider.
⚡ Live updates while doors are open — a compact multi-metric report to the team every 15–120 minutes: people through the gates with pace per hour, bar revenue, top-3 vendors, device health, % of last year — to inbox, push, SMS and WhatsApp.
🚨 Alerts on any number — "tell me when VIP drops below 100": Pulse watches it and taps you on the shoulder the moment it crosses. Cooldowns and quiet hours keep it calm.
