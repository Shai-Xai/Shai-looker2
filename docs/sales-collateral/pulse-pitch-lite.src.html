What Pulse does for you

Six things you'll feel in the first month

🌅 Wake up already knowing

A personal morning briefing and scheduled digests tell you what changed overnight and what's worth doing about it. No digging, no logging in to check.

🦉 Ask your data anything

The Owl is your own AI analyst: in the app, on WhatsApp, even from ChatGPT or Claude. Plain-language questions, grounded answers, only ever from your own data.

⚡ Act in one tap

Reach exactly the right people with on-brand email and SMS, drafted for you, approved by you. From spotting a cohort to a campaign in minutes, not days.

Send Pulse audiences to Meta and TikTok, keep them fresh automatically, and see what the spend actually returned, next to your ticket sales.

🎯 Prove the results

A live command centre for gates, bars and devices, with a compact report to the team's phones through the night. You'll know before the queue does.

One partner, end to end

The Howler One stack: one experience, one dataset

Ticketing, cashless payments, the fan SuperApp and VIP table management, run by one partner, feeding one dataset, wrapped in your brand. Pulse is the brain that turns all of it into decisions.

🎟

Ticketing

The box office: ticket sales, tiers & releases, access control and scanning at the gates, every buyer and every entry, captured from the first on-sale minute.

💳

Cashless Payments

Tap-to-pay across bars, vendors and top-ups: faster queues, higher spend, zero cash risk, and every transaction lands as live data, per station.

📱

SuperApp

The fan's companion: tickets, cashless top-ups, line-ups and offers in one place, a direct, ownable channel to every attendee, before, during and after.

🍾

VIP Table Management

Premium done properly: table inventory, bookings and minimum spends, hosted service, your highest-value guests, managed and measured.
